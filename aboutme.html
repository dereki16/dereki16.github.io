About Me


Hello and welcome to my website!

Born and raised in Southern California, I completed my Bachelors at Cal State University, Monterey Bay with a specialization in Game Development. Programming is my medium of choice for innovation. That passion extends to game, web, and app development.

Having gone through the process of creating websites, apps, and games (as well as one in VR), I am well aware that it takes a lot of work to have a MVP, let alone a completed, polished project.

I want to constantly improve upon my skills and knowledge, and realize there's always going to be another plateau to overcome. However, I believe with a rich learning environment as well as my patience and determination, the possibilities are endless.
